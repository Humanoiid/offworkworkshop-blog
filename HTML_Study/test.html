tags can be applied on the text

* Bold: if you want to bold the text, you can use <strong> and </strong> at the front and back of the text
* underline: if you want to underline the text, you can use <u> and </u> at the front and back of the text
* Heading: heading is make the text as heading. you can use <h1>, h2, h3, ... ,h6 tag at the front and back of the text.
